Input from Novari and HK-dir on potential areas of collaboration related to career guidance and educational pathways.

## Input: Joint project on vilbli.no and utdanning.no/karriereveiledning.no

> Hilde and I have been in dialogue about a possible case related to a potential merger of the services at vilbli.no and utdanning.no/karriereveiledning.no. We have drafted an overview document describing the potential benefits at a high level.
>
> Such a merger project would be more extensive than what could be realised as a case in SAMT-BU, but we are confident that several issues arising from such a project could be strong candidates as cases — for example, a shared login that retrieves data from both Novari's solutions (VIGO) and utdanning.no.
>
> Regarding the input from Sikt, it is important that this is coordinated with HK-dir's ongoing project to develop a chatbot in the career guidance area. We are exploring a model that also makes use of data that is not publicly available (e.g. chat logs from our career guidance service). In that sense, it would be exciting to join forces on such a case.
>
> *Sigurd, Novari*
